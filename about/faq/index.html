Frequently Asked Questions

General Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When was Team 3189 founded?


A: We began competing in FIRST® Robotics Competition in the 2010 season.

Q: What does 3189 even mean?


In the FIRST® Robotics Competition, every team is assigned a number. Our number happens to be 3189.


Q: Is this BattleBots?


Sorry, but no. FRC focuses more on the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) part of robot construction.


EDUHSD Student Frequently Asked Questions

Q: This sounds awesome! What are the requirements to join?


To join the Circuit Breakers, you must be a student at any of EDUHSD's high schools. Additionally, you must be able to attend meetings regularly which are held at the EDUHSD Virtual Academy.


Q: I don't have any previous robotics knowledge, can I still join?


Of course! We have plenty of positions on the team that don't relate to the actual construction of robots. Additionally, we can train anyone for any entry-level position on the team.
