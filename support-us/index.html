Support Us

Our team is always looking for support from members and businesses from the community in any way possible. We rely on students, mentors, and donations from the community to keep us going.

Become a Member

We are always looking for new people to join our team. All you need to join is to be a student of EDUHSD. You don't need any previous knowledge of robotics to join! We welcome students of all fields.

Become a Mentor

In addition to students, we are always looking for people with engineering and business knowledge to mentor. Whether you're a programmer, machinist, electrician, carpenter, painter, analyst, or anything else, we have room for you!

Become a Sponsor

Becoming a sponsor is the one of the easiest ways to support us. Our budget relies solely on the donations we receive from our sponsors, so we greatly appreciate any contributions!
